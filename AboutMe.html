About Me

Developer • Writer • Photographer • Creative Soul

Hello, I'm Om

I am a full-stack developer from Nepal, the country of the Himalayas. I love building things that make a real difference. From crafting smooth and elegant frontend experiences to designing strong and reliable backend systems, I’m always excited about turning ideas into something real.

Beyond coding, I am a passionate writer who enjoys weaving stories and sharing thoughts through words, and a photographer who loves capturing the beauty of moments through my lens.

Creativity, for me, isn’t limited to one field. When I’m not debugging code or working on databases, you’ll often find me exploring new technologies, writing meaningful articles, or wandering with my camera in search of the perfect shot. I enjoy taking on side projects that challenge me and collaborating with others to bring unique ideas to life.

Whether it’s building a modern web application, writing a story that connects with people, or capturing a breathtaking landscape, I approach every project with curiosity, dedication, and the goal of creating something meaningful.

Growing up in Nepal, surrounded by mountains and rich culture, has taught me the value of perspective, patience, and creativity. These qualities inspire me every day, both in technology and in art.

If you have an exciting project in mind, want to collaborate on something creative, or simply want to connect and talk about technology, writing, or photography, I’d love to hear from you. Let’s create something amazing together.
